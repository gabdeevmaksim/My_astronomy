Contents of the directory "X-ray stars" in alphabetical order

ARTXC_unknown.ipynb - correlation of unknown Art_XC objects with SDSS using astroquery. Trying to find color features for different types of objects.
ArtXC_RitKolb.ipynb - period distribution of Art_XC objects correlated with Ritter&Kolb catalogue
Correl_PAN_RTT.ipynb - comparing RTT-150 psf photometry with PANSTARRS.
